About this place

Perched on a dramatic cliff edge with uninterrupted views of the Indian Ocean, this stunning 3-bedroom villa is the ultimate Bali retreat. Wake up to the sound of waves, spend afternoons floating in your private infinity pool, and watch the sunset paint the sky in brilliant oranges and pinks from your outdoor terrace. The villa blends traditional Balinese architecture with modern luxury — carved wooden doors, open-air living spaces, and lush tropical gardens create an atmosphere that is both serene and spectacular. Each bedroom features a king-size bed, premium linens, and its own en-suite bathroom. The fully equipped kitchen and open dining area make it easy to entertain, while the dedicated villa host, Wayan, is available around the clock to make sure every detail of your stay is perfect.
