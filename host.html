FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be home when guests stay? +
Not at all. Many hosts on Hola rent their entire home while they're away, or use smart locks for self check-in. You're in full control of how you host.
How does Hola protect my property? +
Every booking is backed by Hola's Host Guarantee — up to $1,000,000 in property damage protection. We also provide liability insurance so you're covered if a guest is injured.
When and how do I get paid? +
Hola releases your payment 24 hours after your guest checks in. Funds are deposited directly into your nominated bank account, usually arriving within 1–3 business days.
Can I choose who books my place? +
Yes. You can review every guest's profile, read their reviews from other hosts, and accept or decline any booking request. You're never obligated to accept a booking you're not comfortable with.
What are Hola's fees for hosts? +
Hola charges a simple 3% service fee per completed booking — one of the lowest in the industry. There are no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no charges if a booking is cancelled.
Can I host if I'm renting my home? +
It depends on your lease agreement and local laws. We recommend checking with your landlord and local council before listing. Hola provides hosting guides for every major city to help you navigate the rules.
